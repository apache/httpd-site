Apache Platform List

Apache runs on a variety of (UNIX) platforms. In general, an officially supported platform is one where the developers have direct access. Over time, this access has come and gone, but these platforms are still included as "official" Apache ports.

The following is a list of those platforms Apache runs on "out of the box" that have developers who claim "responsibility" for maintaining that port. If you have access to any of those platforms lacking developers, please contact us at apache@apache.org.
